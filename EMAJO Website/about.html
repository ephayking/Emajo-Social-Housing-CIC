Our Mission

A social enterprise built around community benefit

Emajo Social Housing CIC is registered as a Community Interest Company — a legal structure introduced by the UK government in 2005 for organisations that trade with a social purpose.

Every pound we generate is locked in by law to serve our community mission, not private profit. We are accountable to the communities we serve, to our referral partners, and to the CIC Regulator.

What We Stand For

Our core values

Safety First

Every resident deserves to feel safe. We provide fully secured, inspected accommodation with 24/7 support available.

Dignity & Respect

We treat every person as an individual with unique needs, aspirations, and the capacity for change and growth.

Empowerment

We don't just house people — we equip them with the tools, connections, and confidence to rebuild their lives.

Transparency

Our annual reports are public. Our work is open. We hold ourselves accountable to every resident, partner, and regulator.

Our Journey

How we got here

Founded
Emajo CIC is established

Founded by a team of social care professionals with a shared vision: to reduce reoffending through stable, supported housing for people leaving the justice system.

Year One
First residents housed

We welcomed our first residents into fully furnished, move-in ready accommodation, partnering with local probation services to ensure smooth transitions.

Growing
Expanding our partnerships

Relationships established with NHS trusts, local authorities, and charities to deliver wraparound support — from mental health to employment pathways.

Today
A growing community of hope

We continue to expand, deepen our impact, and advocate for systemic change — because housing is just the beginning.
