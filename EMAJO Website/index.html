Why Housing Matters

The evidence is clear. Housing saves lives and protects communities.

  • Supported Housing

    Safe, stable accommodation tailored to the needs of people transitioning out of the justice system.

  • Rehabilitation Support

    Wraparound casework connecting residents to employment, mental health services, and community.

  • Partnership Working

    We work closely with probation services, prisons, and local authorities to ensure seamless transitions.

Our Purpose

A social enterprise built around community benefit

Emajo Social Housing CIC is registered as a Community Interest Company — a legal structure introduced by the UK government in 2005 for organisations that trade with a social purpose. Every pound we generate is locked in by law to serve our community mission, not private profit.

We are accountable to the communities we serve, to our referral partners, and to the CIC Regulator. Our annual reports are public, and our work is transparent.
