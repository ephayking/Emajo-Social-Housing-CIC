Referrals & Applications

Start someone's
new chapter today with Emajo

We welcome referrals from probation officers, prison staff, social workers, and other professionals working in criminal justice and resettlement.

Who is eligible for our support?

  • Adults (18+) leaving custody or on licence
  • Individuals at risk of homelessness following a crisis
  • People with a history of substance use seeking a fresh start
  • Vulnerable adults requiring a supported living environment
  • Individuals referred by statutory or voluntary agencies

Who can make a referral?

We accept referrals from a broad range of statutory and voluntary organisations. If you're working with someone who needs stable, supported housing, we want to hear from you.

Common Questions

Referral FAQs

We aim to acknowledge all referrals within 24 hours and provide a full response within 2 working days. For urgent cases, please call us directly.
We primarily accept referrals from statutory and voluntary agencies. However, if you are seeking support for yourself, please get in touch via our contact page and we will do our best to help.
We currently operate across several regions in the UK. Contact us to discuss whether we have suitable accommodation in your area.
There is no cost to the referring agency. Accommodation costs are typically covered through housing benefit, local authority commissioning, or other funding arrangements. We can advise on funding routes.
Our team reviews the referral and assesses whether the individual meets our eligibility criteria and whether we have suitable availability. We will contact you directly to discuss next steps, including a needs assessment.
